I'm glad to be able to serve individuals and communities by offering the following services.

1 Subject Matter Expertise
I have actively worked as a subject matter expert in both academia and industry under my areas of expertise. I have delivered many technical reports on my work and experience to both my peer experts in the field and to the general public. I am excited to translate deep technical ideas and new concepts across all organizational levels. You can find links to some of my written reports in writings page [in which most of them target general public] and publications page [in which they target subject matter experts]. For more information, please contact me.

2 Technical/Introductional Presentations
I have delivered various technical presentations on my research in national and international seminars, workshops, and conferences since 2008. I have also given invited talks in different universities and institutes to a broad range of audience in several occasions. I am willing to continue this path and keep sharing my knowledge to interested audience. Please contact me if you like to have me for delivering a technical/introductional presentation to your community.

3 Free Consultancy to Juniors
I am happy to obligate myself to spend up to two hours each week to communicate and work with motivated, young individuals to share my knowledge and expertise. You can ask me on any topic you think I can help you with. It could be anything from requesting for reviewing your CV that you are preparing for a job/grad-school application all the way through a specific question under my areas of expertise that could be useful in your own work. I will be delighted if I can be a help. To use this service, please send me an email [with subject "inquiry@tsp"] including (1) a brief introduction about yourself, and (2) a brief description on how I can help you. Please be specific.

4 Peer Review Service
In my free time, I enjoy peer reviewing for scientific journals under my areas of expertise. It has been my pleasure to be selected as a referee by the editorial boards of multiple top scientific journals from USA and Europe since 2014.
